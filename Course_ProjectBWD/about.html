Varesa is an orchard owner who lives with the tribe the Collective of Plenty, which gets its name from the fertile land they live on as it is neighboring a volcano. Varesa is typically characterised by her hard working attitude, shy demeanor, and insatiable appitite. Varesa also dreams to be a hero she dons her mask which helps give her courage to fight monsters.
